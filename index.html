The Hobbit

Summary

In The Hobbit, Bilbo Baggins, a peace-loving hobbit, get pulled in an adventure to recapture the dwarves homeland from a Dragon.

My Thoughts

This book is a very enjoyable read. The quick interrupts of the story by the author adds comedic bits and gives the book character. The story is also a very simple romp and does not have much story depth or even much character depth as far as I can see.
